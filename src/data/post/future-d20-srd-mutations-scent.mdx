## 
    Scent [MINOR]
   



    You can detect approaching enemies, sniff out hidden foes, and track by sense of smell. You can also identify familiar odors the way humans do familiar sights.
   



    MP Cost: 2.
   



    Benefit: You gain the scent special ability.
