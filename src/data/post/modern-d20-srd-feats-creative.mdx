## Creative




    Benefit: Pick two of the following skills:
    [Craft  (visual art)](modern-d20-srd-skills-craft-visual-art)
,
[Craft (writing)](modern-d20-srd-skills-craft-writing)
,
[Perform (act)](modern-d20-srd-skills-perform)
,
[Perform (dance)](modern-d20-srd-skills-perform)
,
[Perform (keyboards)](modern-d20-srd-skills-perform)
,
[Perform (percussion instruments)](modern-d20-srd-skills-perform)
,
[Perform (sing)](modern-d20-srd-skills-perform)
,
[Perform (stand-up)](modern-d20-srd-skills-perform)
,
[Perform (string instruments)](modern-d20-srd-skills-perform)
, and
[Perform (wind instruments)](modern-d20-srd-skills-perform)
. The character gets a +2 bonus on all checks with those two skills.

    Special: A character can select this feat as many as five times. Each time, the character selects two new skills from the choices given above.
