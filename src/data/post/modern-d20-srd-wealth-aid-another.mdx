##

     Aid Another




     One other character can make an aid another attempt to help a character purchase an object or service. If the attempt is successful, that character provides the purchaser with a +2 bonus on his or her
     [Wealth  check](modern-d20-srd-wealth-wealth-check)
. The character who provides the aid reduces his or her
[Wealth bonus](modern-d20-srd-wealth-wealth-bonus)
by +1.
