## White Collar




     Office workers and desk jockeys, lawyers, accountants, insurance agents, bank personnel, financial advisors, tax preparers, clerks, sales personnel, real estate agents, and a variety of mid-level managers fall within the scope of this starting occupation.




     Prerequisite: Age 23+.




     Skills: Choose two of the following
     [skills](modern-d20-srd-skills-index)
     as permanent class
     [skills](modern-d20-srd-skills-index)
     . If a
     [skill](modern-d20-srd-skills-index)
     the character selects is already a class
     [skill](modern-d20-srd-skills-index)
     , he or she receives a +1 competence bonus on checks using that
     [skill](modern-d20-srd-skills-index)
     .

[Computer Use](modern-d20-srd-skills-computer-use)
,
[Diplomacy](modern-d20-srd-skills-diplomacy)
,
[Knowledge](modern-d20-srd-skills-knowledge)
(art, business, civics, earth and life
sciences, history, physical sciences, or technology),
[Research](modern-d20-srd-skills-research)
.

[Wealth Bonus](modern-d20-srd-wealth-wealth-bonus)
Increase: +3.
