## 
    Multiplying Damage
   



    Sometimes
    [
     damage
    ](modern-d20-srd-combat-damage)
    is multiplied by some factor. Roll the
    [
     damage
    ](modern-d20-srd-combat-damage)
    (with all modifiers) multiple times and total the results.
   



    Bonus
    [
     damage
    ](modern-d20-srd-combat-damage)
    represented as extra dice is an exception. Do not multiply bonus
    [
     damage
    ](modern-d20-srd-combat-damage)
    dice when a character scores a
    [
     critical
  hit
    ](modern-d20-srd-combat-critical-hits)
    .
