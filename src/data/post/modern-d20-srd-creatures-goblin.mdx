## Goblin

### Species Traits

Skill Bonus: Goblins gain a +4 species bonus on [Move Silently](modern-d20-srd-skills-move-silently) checks.

Bonus Feat: Goblins are keenly aware of their surroundings and receive the bonus feat [Alertness](modern-d20-srd-feats-alertness).

Automatic Language: Goblins [read](modern-d20-srd-skills-read-write-language), [write](modern-d20-srd-skills-read-write-language), and [speak](modern-d20-srd-skills-speak-language) Goblin.
