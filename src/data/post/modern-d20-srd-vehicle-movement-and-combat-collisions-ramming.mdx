## Collisions and Ramming




    A collision occurs when a
    [vehicle](modern-d20-srd-equipment-equipment-vehicles)
    strikes another
    [vehicle](modern-d20-srd-equipment-equipment-vehicles)
    or a solid object. Generally, when a
    [vehicle](modern-d20-srd-equipment-equipment-vehicles)

[collides](modern-d20-srd-vehicle-movement-and-combat-resolving-collisions)
with a creature or other moving vehicle, the target can attempt a
[Reflex save](modern-d20-srd-basics-saving-throws)
(DC 15) to reduce the damage by half.
