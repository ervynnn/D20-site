##

     Wounding Handgun




     Any bullet fired from this magic
     [Glock     17](modern-d20-srd-equipment-weapons-handguns)
     deals such a terrible wound that, in addition to taking normal
     [damage](modern-d20-srd-combat-damage)
     , the victim bleeds for 1 point of
     [damage](modern-d20-srd-combat-damage)
     each round thereafter. Multiple wounds from the handgun result in cumulative bleeding loss. The bleeding can only be stopped with a successful
     [Treat     Injury](modern-d20-srd-skills-treat-injury)

[check](modern-d20-srd-skills-skill-basics-php#skill-check-index)
(DC 15) or the application of a healing spell.

     Type: Weapon (magic); Caster Level: 10th; Purchase DC: 38 (+1), 43 (+2), 48 (+3); Weight: 2 lb.
