## 
    Extra Digits [COSMETIC]
   



    You gain an extra digit on each hand and foot. The extra fingers or toes, although fully functional, do not confer any special benefit, nor do they hinder you in any way.
   



    MP Cost: 0.
   



    Benefit: None.
