## Radioactive [MAJOR]


You are immune to some radiation and can emit bursts of harmful radiation from your body.


MP Cost: 5.


Benefits: You are immune to mild, low, and moderate degrees of radiation exposure. In addition, your body acts as a radiation battery, storing the energy for later use. Once per day as a free action, you may release a 60-foot-radius burst of radiation centered on you.
