##

     Illusory Concealable Vest




     The wearer of this concealable vest gains a +1 to +3 enchantment bonus to
     [Defense](modern-d20-srd-combat-defense)
     . Upon command, the protective garment changes shape and form to assume the appearance of a sweater or other normal piece of
     [clothing](modern-d20-srd-equipment-clothing)
     . The vest retains all its properties (including weight) when its illusion ability is in effect. Only a
     [true     seeing](modern-d20-srd-fx-true-seeing)
     spell reveals the true nature of the armor.




     Type:
     [Armor](modern-d20-srd-fx-items-armor)
     (magic); Caster Level: 10th; Purchase DC: 31 (+1), 36 (+2), 41 (+3); Weight: 4 lb.
