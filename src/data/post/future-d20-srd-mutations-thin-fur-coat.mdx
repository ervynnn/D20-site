## 
    Thin Fur Coat [COSMETIC]
   



    You grow a thin coat of brown or golden-brown fur, similar to that of a small mammal.
   



    MP Cost: 0.
   



    Benefit: None.
   



    Special: A creature with fur, scales, or chitin cannot gain this mutation.
