##

    Wealth and the Starting Hero




    A newly created 1st-level character’s
    [Wealth  bonus](modern-d20-srd-wealth-wealth-bonus)
is +0 plus:

- Wealth provided by the character’s
  [starting   occupation](modern-d20-srd-starting-occupation-index)
  .
- Bonus from the
  [Windfall](modern-d20-srd-feats-windfall)
  feat, if taken.
- 2d4 die roll.
- +1 for having 1 to 4 ranks in the
  [Profession](modern-d20-srd-skills-profession)
  skill.
