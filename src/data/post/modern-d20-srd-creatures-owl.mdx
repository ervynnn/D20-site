## Owl

### Species Traits

Skill Bonuses: Owls receive a +8 species bonus on [Listen](modern-d20-srd-skills-listen) checks and a +14 species bonus on [Move Silently](modern-d20-srd-skills-move-silently) checks. They receive a +8 species bonus on [Spot](modern-d20-srd-skills-spot) checks in dusk and darkness.

Bonus Feat: Owls gain the bonus feat [Weapon Finesse](modern-d20-srd-feats-weapon-finesse) (claw).
