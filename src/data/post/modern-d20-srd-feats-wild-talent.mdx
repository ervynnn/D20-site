##  Wild Talent

Benefit: Select one 0-level [ psionic ](modern-d20-srd-psionics-index) power.
The character can manifest this power up to three times in a 24-hour period.
There is no power point cost for using this power. For effects that depends on
manifester level the character is considered 1st level or his or her
manifester level, whichever is higher.
