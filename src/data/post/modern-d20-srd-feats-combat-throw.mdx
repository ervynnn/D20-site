##  Combat Throw

Prerequisite: [ Defensive Martial Arts ](modern-d20-srd-feats-defensive-martial-arts) .

Benefit: The character gains a +2 bonus on opposed [ Strength](modern-d20-srd-basics-ability-scores) and [ Dexterity ](modern-d20-srd-basics-ability-scores) checks any time the character attempts [ trip](modern-d20-srd-combat-trip) or [ grapple ](modern-d20-srd-combat-grapple)
attacks, or when the character trys to avoid a [ trip ](modern-d20-srd-combat-trip) or [ grapple ](modern-d20-srd-combat-grapple) attack made against him or
her.
