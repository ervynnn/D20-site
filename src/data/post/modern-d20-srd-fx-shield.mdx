## Shield




    Abjuration [Force]

[Level](modern-d20-srd-fx-level)
:
[Mage](modern-d20-srd-classes-advanced-mage)
1;
[Components](modern-d20-srd-fx-components)
: V, S;
[Casting Time](modern-d20-srd-fx-casting-time)
;
[Attack action](modern-d20-srd-combat-attack-actions)
[Range](modern-d20-srd-fx-range)

[Target](modern-d20-srd-fx-target)
: You;
[Duration](modern-d20-srd-fx-duration)
: 1 minute/level (D)

    Shield creates an invisible, mobile disk of force that hovers in front of

the caster. It negates magic missile attacks directed at the caster. The disk
also intercepts attacks, providing a +4 bonus to
[Defense](modern-d20-srd-combat-damage)
. The shield carries
no armor penalty or arcane spell failure chance.
