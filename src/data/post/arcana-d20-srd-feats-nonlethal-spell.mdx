##

     Nonlethal Spell [Metamagic]




     You can modify a spell to deal nonlethal instead of lethal damage.





      Benefit:

     You may modify a spell that deals lethal damage to deal nonlethal damage instead. All other effects of the spell remain normal (for example, a nonlethal fireball may still set flammable objects ablaze).
