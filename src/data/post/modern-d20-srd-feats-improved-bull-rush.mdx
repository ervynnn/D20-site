##  Improved Bull Rush

Prerequisites: [ Strength ](modern-d20-srd-basics-ability-scores) 13, [ Power Attack ](modern-d20-srd-feats-power-attack) .

Benefit: When the character performs a [ bull rush ](modern-d20-srd-combat-bull-rush) , the character does not provoke an [ attack of opportunity](modern-d20-srd-combat-attacks-of-opportunity) from the defender.
