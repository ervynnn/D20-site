## Shark

### Species Traits

Aquatic: Sharks can move in water without making [Swim](modern-d20-srd-skills-swim) checks and cannot drown in water.

Keen [Scent](modern-d20-srd-special-abilities-scent) (Ex): A shark can notice creatures by [scent](modern-d20-srd-special-abilities-scent) in a 180-foot radius and detect blood in the water at ranges of up to one mile.

Bonus Feat: Sharks gain the bonus feat [Weapon Finesse](modern-d20-srd-feats-weapon-finesse) (bite).
