##

    Smoke




    Characters breathing heavy smoke or similar toxic gases must make a
    [
     Constitution
    ](modern-d20-srd-basics-ability-scores)
    check (DC 10, +1 for each previous check) each round or spend that round choking and coughing. Characters who choke for 2 consecutive rounds take 1d6 points of
    [
     damage
    ](modern-d20-srd-combat-damage)
    .




    Smoke also obscures vision, giving one-half
    [
     concealment
    ](modern-d20-srd-combat-concealment)
    (20% miss chance) to characters within it.
