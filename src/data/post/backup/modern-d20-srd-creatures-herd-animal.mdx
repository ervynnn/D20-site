## Herd Animal

### Species Traits

Stampede (Ex): A frightened herd flees as a group in a random direction, but always away from the perceived source of danger. It runs over anything of size Large or smaller that gets in its way, dealing 1d12 points of [damage](modern-d20-srd-combat-damage) for every five animals in the herd. A successful Reflex [save](modern-d20-srd-basics-saving-throws) (DC 16) halves the [damage](modern-d20-srd-combat-damage).
