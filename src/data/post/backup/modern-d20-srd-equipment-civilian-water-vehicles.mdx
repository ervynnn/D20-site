Piloting a water vehicle is covered by the [Drive](modern-d20-srd-skills-drive) skill.

### Bayliner 1802 Capri

This is a large runabouta powerboat with an outboard engine and an open cockpit with a tiny cabin (about the size of the interior of an economy car) forward. It comes with a trailer; loading or unloading it requires a paved boat ramp and 10 minutes of work. The Capri provides one-half [cover](modern-d20-srd-combat-cover) to occupants in the cockpit or stern, full [cover](modern-d20-srd-combat-cover) to occupants in the cabin, and no [cover](modern-d20-srd-combat-cover) to those forward of the cockpit. The Capri is two squares wide and four squares long.

### Fairline Targa 30

This cabin cruiser is a motor yacht with two internal diesel engines. It comes equipped with four berths and a fully equipped galley. It provides one-half [cover](modern-d20-srd-combat-cover) to occupants in the cockpit or stern, full [cover](modern-d20-srd-combat-cover) to occupants below deck, and no [cover](modern-d20-srd-combat-cover) to those forward of the cockpit. The Targa is three squares wide and six squares long.

### Sea-Doo XP

This is a two-seat jet ski that propels itself with a powerful jet of water. The Sea-Doo XP is one square wide and two squares long, and provides no [cover](modern-d20-srd-combat-cover) for its riders.
