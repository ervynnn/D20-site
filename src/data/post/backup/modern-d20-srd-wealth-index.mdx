## Wealth

Every character has a [Wealth ](modern-d20-srd-wealth-wealth-bonus)[bonus](modern-d20-srd-wealth-wealth-bonus) that reflects his or her buying powera composite of income, credit rating, and savings. A characters [Wealth
bonus](modern-d20-srd-wealth-wealth-bonus) serves as the basis of the characters [Wealth
check](modern-d20-srd-wealth-wealth-check), which is used to purchase [equipment](modern-d20-srd-equipment-equipment-general) and [services](modern-d20-srd-equipment-services) for the character.
