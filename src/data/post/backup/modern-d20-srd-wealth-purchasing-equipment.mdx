##

    Purchasing Equipment

[
Wealth checks
](modern-d20-srd-wealth-wealth-check)
are used to determine what characters can afford and what
[
gear
](modern-d20-srd-equipment-index)
they might reasonably have access to. Every character has a
[
Wealth
bonus
](modern-d20-srd-wealth-wealth-bonus)
that reflects his or her buying power. Every
[
object
](modern-d20-srd-equipment-equipment-general)
and
[
service
](modern-d20-srd-equipment-services)
has a purchase DC. To purchase an
[
object
](modern-d20-srd-equipment-equipment-general)
, make a
[
Wealth
check
](modern-d20-srd-wealth-wealth-check)
against the purchase DC.
