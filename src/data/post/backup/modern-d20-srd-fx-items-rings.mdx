##

     Rings




     A ring is a circular metal band worn on the finger that contains a
     [
      spell-like
     ](modern-d20-srd-fx-index)
     ability or a
     [
      psionic

power
](modern-d20-srd-psionics-index)
(often an effect that persists as long as the ring is worn). A ring has a Defense of 13, hardness 10, 2 hit points, and a break DC of 25.

     Activating a ring is an
     [
      attack

action
](modern-d20-srd-combat-attack-actions)
and does not provoke
[
attacks
of opportunity
](modern-d20-srd-combat-attacks-of-opportunity)
.

     Purchase DC: A rings purchase DC is 25 + the rings caster level unless noted otherwise.
