## Survival (Wis)

[Check](modern-d20-srd-skills-skill-basics-php#skill-check-index): A character can keep his or herself and others safe and fed in the wild.

| DC                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    |                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              Task |
| :-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------: |
| 10                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    | Get along in the wild. Move up to half the characters overland speed while hunting and foraging (no food or water supplies needed). The character can provide food and water for one other person for every 2 points by which the characters [check](modern-d20-srd-skills-skill-basics-php#skill-check-index) result exceeds 10. |

The character may grant the same bonus to one other character for every 1 point by which the characters [check](modern-d20-srd-skills-skill-basics-php#skill-check-index) result exceeds 15. |
| 18                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    |                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  Avoid getting lost and avoid natural hazards, such as quicksand. |

With the [Track](modern-d20-srd-feats-track) feat, a character can use Survival [checks](modern-d20-srd-skills-skill-basics-php#skill-check-index) to track a character or animal across various terrain types.

Special: A character can [take
10](modern-d20-srd-skills-skill-basics-php#take10-index) when making a Survival [check](modern-d20-srd-skills-skill-basics-php#skill-check-index). A character can [take
20](modern-d20-srd-skills-skill-basics-php#take20-index) when tracking, or if there is no danger or penalty for failure, but not on periodic [checks](modern-d20-srd-skills-skill-basics-php#skill-check-index) to get along in the wild.

A character with the [Guide](modern-d20-srd-feats-guide) feat gets a +2 bonus on all Survival [checks](modern-d20-srd-skills-skill-basics-php#skill-check-index).

Time: Basic Survival checks occur each day in the wilderness or whenever a hazard presents itself. When using Survival with the [Track](modern-d20-srd-feats-track) feat to track a character or animal, [checks](modern-d20-srd-skills-skill-basics-php#skill-check-index) are made according to distance, as described in the [Track](modern-d20-srd-feats-track) feat.
