## Advanced Combat Martial Arts




    Prerequisites:
    [Combat  Martial Arts](modern-d20-srd-feats-combat-martial-arts)
,
[Improved Combat Martial Arts](modern-d20-srd-feats-improved-combat-martial-arts)
, base attack bonus +8.

    Benefit: When the character scores a
    [critical  hit](modern-d20-srd-combat-critical-hits)
on an opponent with an unarmed strike, the character deals triple
[damage](modern-d20-srd-combat-damage)
.

    Normal: An unarmed strike
    [critical  hit](modern-d20-srd-combat-critical-hits)
deals double
[damage](modern-d20-srd-combat-damage)
.
