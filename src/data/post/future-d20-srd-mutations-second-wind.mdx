## 
    Second Wind [MINOR]
   



    You can shrug off minor wounds with ease.
   



    MP Cost: 2.
   



    Benefit: Once per day, as a free action, you can heal yourself of a number of points of damage equal to your Constitution modifier (minimum 1).
