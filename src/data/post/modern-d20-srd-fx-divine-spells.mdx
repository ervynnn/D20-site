## Divine Spells

Unlike arcane spells, divine spells draw power from the strength of the spellcasters beliefs. Divine spells tend to be less flashy, destructive, and disruptive than [arcane spells](modern-d20-srd-fx-arcane-spells). What they do that [arcane spells](modern-d20-srd-fx-arcane-spells) dont do is heal.
