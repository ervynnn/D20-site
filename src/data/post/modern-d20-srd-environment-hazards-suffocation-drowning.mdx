## Suffocation and Drowning

A character in an airless environment (underwater, vacuum) can hold his or her breath for a number of rounds equal to his or her [Constitution](modern-d20-srd-basics-ability-scores) score. After this period of time, the character must make a [Constitution check](modern-d20-srd-basics-ability-scores) (DC 10) every round to continue holding his or her breath. Each round, the DC of the [Constitution](modern-d20-srd-basics-ability-scores) check increases by 1.

When the character fails one of these [Constitution](modern-d20-srd-basics-ability-scores) checks, he or she begins to suffocate or drown.
