## Cover




     When a character fires from a
     [vehicle](modern-d20-srd-equipment-equipment-vehicles)
     , objects or other
     [vehicles](modern-d20-srd-equipment-equipment-vehicles)
     in the way can provide
     [cover](modern-d20-srd-combat-cover)
     for the target.
