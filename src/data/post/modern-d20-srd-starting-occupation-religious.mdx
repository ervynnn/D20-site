##

     Religious




     Ordained clergy of all persuasions, as well as theological scholars and experts on religious studies fall within the scope of this starting occupation.




     Prerequisite: Age 23+.




     Skills: Choose three of the following
     [skills](modern-d20-srd-skills-index)
     as permanent class
     [skills](modern-d20-srd-skills-index)
     . If a
     [skill](modern-d20-srd-skills-index)
     the character selects is already a class
     [skill](modern-d20-srd-skills-index)
     , he or she receives a +1 competence bonus on checks using that
     [skill](modern-d20-srd-skills-index)
     .

[Decipher Script](modern-d20-srd-skills-decipher-script)
,
[Knowledge](modern-d20-srd-skills-knowledge)
(arcane lore, art, behavioral sciences, history,
streetwise, or theology and philosophy),
[Listen](modern-d20-srd-skills-listen)
,
[Sense Motive](modern-d20-srd-skills-sense-motive)
.

[Wealth Bonus](modern-d20-srd-wealth-wealth-bonus)
Increase: +2.
