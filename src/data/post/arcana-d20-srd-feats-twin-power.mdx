## Twin Power [Metapsionic]


You can manifest a power simultaneously with another power just like it.


Benefit: Manifesting a power altered by this feat causes the power to take effect twice on the target, as if you were simultaneously manifesting the same power two times on the same location or target. Any variables in the power (such as targets, shaping an area, and so on) apply to both of the resulting powers. The target suffers all the effects of both powers individually and receives a saving throw for each. A twinned power costs a number of power points equal to its standard cost +8.
