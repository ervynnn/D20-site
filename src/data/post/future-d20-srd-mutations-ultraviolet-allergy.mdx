## 
    Ultraviolet Allergy [DRAWBACK]
   



    Ultraviolet light burns your flesh and causes it to ignite.
   



    MP Value: 3.
   



    Drawback: Ultraviolet light (including direct sunlight) burns you for 2d6 points of fire damage per round and causes any light, flammable clothing you are
wearing to ignite.
