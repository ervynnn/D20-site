## Charismatic Plus


Advancements in science coupled with your drive to excel enable you to progress faster toward becoming a truly charismatic hero.


Benefit: You gain two talents from the Charismatic hero's talent trees. The talents must be selected from the following list, you cannot
select more than one talent from a single talent tree, and you must meet all the prerequisites of a talent to select it. Favor, Captivate,
Dazzle, Taunt, Inspiration, Greater Inspiration.


Special: You may select this feat multiple times. Each time you select this feat, you must choose a different pair of talents.
