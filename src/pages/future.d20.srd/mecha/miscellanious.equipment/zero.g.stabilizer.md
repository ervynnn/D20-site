A zero-G stabilizer allows the mecha and its operator to function normally in
low-gravity and zero-gravity environments, as though the operator has the
Zero-G Training feat (see page 15).

**Equipment Slots** : 1.  
**Activation** : None.  
**Range** : Personal.  
**Target** : You.  
**Duration** : Persistent.  
**Saving Throw** : None.  
**Purchase DC** : 15.  
**Restriction** : None.
